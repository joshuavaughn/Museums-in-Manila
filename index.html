Museums in Manila

Discover Manila's museums with our guide! Explore history, art, and culture, complete with location maps to plan your visit effortlessly.

Anthropology

The National Museum of Anthropology in Manila, formerly the Museum of the Filipino People, showcases ethnological and archaeological exhibits. Located in Agrifina Circle, Rizal Park, it is part of the National Museum complex.

Fine Arts

The National Museum of Fine Arts in Manila, formerly the National Art Gallery, showcases works by classical Filipino artists like Juan Luna and Guillermo Tolentino. Located on Padre Burgos Avenue near Rizal Park, it is part of the National Museum complex.

Natural History

The National Museum of Natural History (Filipino: Pambansang Museo ng Likas na Kasaysayan) is the national natural history museum of the Philippines. It is located along Agrifina Circle in Rizal Park, Manila.
