Hyundai Hall

Hyundai Philippines Entrance Hall is the main entrance to the National Museum of Natural History. Its grandeur and timeless design was restored faithfully to the aim described by the original 1939 architectural drawings. It now houses the Marinduque Sperm whale skeleton where visitors can appreciate the specimen while waiting to be admitted inside.

Philippine Biodiversity

The NMNH is designed thematically to tackle the dynamic systems of our Natural Heritage from the highest mountains to the deepest seas. You will begin that journey in this gallery where we introduce the various dynamics that shaped, and is still shaping, our biological diversity.

Minerals and Energy Resources

This gallery, divided into two sections, features the different types of natural resources that we utilize in the Philippines. It builds awareness on the origin of these resources, how they are obtained and how they contribute to our everyday well-being.
